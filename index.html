Timeline

(DAY 1) Digital Comprehensive Analysis for Agriculture Industry with Ci-Agriculture

Speaker: Regi Wahyu, Founder of CI-Agriculture

Through a big data analysis approach, CI-Agriculture (a subsidiary of Mediatrac) developed an agricultural management system that is capable of producing comprehensive analysis based on weather analysis, soil sensor information, as well as satellite and drone imaging that can increase agricultural productivity. The system developed is arranged in three products, namely Crop Accurate, Agritrack and Crop Insurance.

(DAY 2) Robotic Technology in Cigarette Production with PT Djarum

Speaker: Robert Budi Hartono, Founder of PT Djarum

PT Djarum admits using robots or robotization in the cigarette production process at their factory in Kudus, Central Java. This technology has been used for 4 years by PT Djarum. This is part of keeping up with technological developments.

(DAY 3) Technology for Fundraising Foundation with Kitabisa.com

Speaker: Raymundus Galih Prasetya, Chief of Technology of Kitabisa.com

Founded in 2013, the Kitabisa website and application have become a bridge for goodness and a forum for mutual assistance for the Indonesian people. Currently KItabisa.com has helped the funding process reach 1.5 million transactions, and oversees 3000 social institutions, all because of the technology it uses.
